THAMARVEL

A Recoding artiste

Thamarvel was born and brought up in Nigeria, he drives his root and origin from Ondo State where He also started his education at Brighter school in Ikare, He continued his high school at Greater Tomorrow College and completed his first degree at the age of 21 from Ondo state University.

After having a very good run with singles like Foreplay featuring Magnito and ClassiQ and his recently released single High Budget featuring Promphizy. Nigerian singer and songwriter, Thamarvel releases a brand new studio project tagged How It All Started Volume 1 The album is a 9 tracks project which houses songs like “Most Wanted” featuring Abuja-based rapper, Psycho YP, Slow and Steady featuring Sassy, Caesar To Caesar featuring Malas_sa Woman, To The Ones That Showed Love and many more. Production credits for the album goes to Milez, Fuego, Tiztext and Geekbeatz.
